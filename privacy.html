Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

Bharat Nova AI ("we," "us," or "our") provides a multi-engine AI chatbot application that lets you converse with several third-party AI language models through a single interface (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use the Service, how we use and protect it, and the choices available to you. By creating an account or otherwise using the Service, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

Account information. When you sign up, we collect your name and email address through Firebase Authentication so we can create and secure your account.

Conversations and uploads. We process the text messages you type, and any images or files you choose to attach, in order to generate a response. This content is sent to the AI engine you select, or to an automatic fallback engine if your chosen engine is unavailable.

Local chat history. Your conversation history is stored in your browser's local storage on your own device so you can revisit past chats. We do not keep a separate server-side archive of your conversations beyond what is technically required to generate a response.

Usage and device data. Like most web applications, we automatically receive technical information such as your IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, and timestamps, collected through standard server logs and cookies.

Cookies and similar technologies. We use cookies and local storage to keep you signed in, remember your preferred AI engine, and support core functionality of the Service.

2. How We Use Information

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the Service
  • To authenticate your account and keep it secure
  • To route your messages and attachments to the selected AI engine and return a response
  • To diagnose technical issues and monitor the reliability of our automatic fallback system
  • To respond to support requests sent through our Contact page
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations

3. Third-Party AI Processing

Bharat Nova AI works by sending your prompts and any attached images to one or more independent third-party AI providers, which currently may include Google Gemini, Groq, OpenRouter, Cohere, Fireworks AI, NVIDIA NIM, and Hugging Face. These providers process your input solely to generate a response and operate under their own respective privacy policies. We do not control how these third parties handle data once it is processed on their infrastructure, and we encourage you to review their individual privacy practices if you have specific concerns.

4. Advertising and Cookies

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements within the Service. Google and its advertising partners use cookies — including the DART cookie — to serve ads based on your visits to this site and other sites on the internet. This helps support free access to the Service.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings, or by visiting www.aboutads.info/choices and the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page.

5. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • The AI engine providers listed in Section 3, strictly to generate responses to your prompts
  • Firebase and Google Cloud, our authentication and hosting infrastructure provider
  • Google AdSense and analytics partners, to serve and measure advertising performance
  • Law enforcement or regulators, where required to comply with applicable law

6. Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account remains active. Conversation content stored locally in your browser stays on your device until you delete it or clear your browser storage. Server-side logs used for security and diagnostics are retained for a limited period and then automatically purged.

7. Data Security

We use industry-standard safeguards, including Firebase Authentication and encrypted HTTPS connections, to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

9. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information, or to object to certain processing. You can delete individual chats or your entire chat history directly within the app at any time, and you can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us using the details below.

10. International Data Transfers

Because our service providers operate global infrastructure, your information may be processed in countries other than your own. We take reasonable steps to ensure such transfers are handled in line with applicable data protection laws.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Your continued use of the Service after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data rights, please reach out via our Contact page or email us at support@bharatnovaai.app.
